Game Rules

Blackjack Rules

Learn how to play Blackjack:

  • Get as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.
  • Face cards are worth 10, and Aces can be 1 or 11.
  • Beat the dealer's hand or make the dealer bust to win.
  • Both the dealer and player start with two cards, and you can choose to hit or stand.

Roulette Rules

Learn how to play Roulette:

  • The game features a spinning wheel along with a ball.
  • Players place bets on where they think the ball will land when the wheel stops spinning.
  • If the ball lands on the color you bet on, you win according to the payout odds.
